Titre: 💖 FANAMBARANA FITIAVANA

1. I love you more than anything in the world.
   Traduction: Tiako ianao mihoatra noho ny zava-drehetra eto amin'izao tontolo izao.

2. Your smile brightens my days.
   Traduction: Ny tsikiminao no mamirapiratra ny androko.

3. I think of you every second.
   Traduction: Mieritreritra anao aho isaky ny segondra.

4. You are the woman of my life.
   Traduction: Ianao no vehivavy amin'ny fiainako.

Titre: 🌙 TONONKALO FITIAVANA

1. In your eyes I see infinity.
   Traduction: Ao anaty masonao no ahitako ny tsy manam-petra.

2. Your love is my refuge.
   Traduction: Ny fitiavanao no fialofako.
