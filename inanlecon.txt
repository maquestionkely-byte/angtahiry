  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "which" sy "what" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny teny <strong>"which"</strong> sy <strong>"what"</strong> dia <strong>mpamaritra (determiners)</strong> ampiasaina amin'ny <strong>fanontaniana</strong> sy ny <strong>fanehoana fahagagana</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia <strong>miaraka amin'ny anarana (nom)</strong> mba hamaritana azy.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"which"</strong> dia midika hoe <strong>"iza amin'ireo"</strong> amin'ny teny malagasy.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"what"</strong> dia midika hoe <strong>"inona"</strong> na <strong>"karazana inona"</strong> amin'ny teny malagasy.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Which</strong> → ampiasaina rehefa <strong>misy safidy voafetra</strong> (fantatra ny lisitra) ary mila misafidy.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>What</strong> → ampiasaina rehefa <strong>tsy misy fetra</strong> ny safidy, na rehefa mangataka fampahalalana momba ny zavatra iray.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 1. WHICH – DÉTERMINANT INTERROGATIF                          -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "WHICH" – DÉTERMINANT INTERROGATIF <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Iza amin'ireo)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>WHICH</strong> + <strong>nom</strong> + <strong>verbe</strong> ?<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      Mampiasa <strong>"which"</strong> isika rehefa <strong>misy safidy voafetra</strong> (fantatra ny lisitra) ary mila <strong>misafidy</strong> amin'izy ireo.
    </div>

    <!-- ====== Exemples WHICH ====== -->
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🔍</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">

      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> colour do you prefer? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo loko</strong> no tianao kokoa?</span>

      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> teacher is your favourite? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo mpampianatra</strong> no tianao indrindra?</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> car is yours? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo fiara</strong> no anao?</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> way should we go? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo lalana</strong> no tokony halehantsika?</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> dress should I wear? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo akanjo</strong> no tokony hataoko?</span>

      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> restaurant do you like? → <strong>Iza amin'ireo trano fisakafoanana</strong> no tianao?</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ampiasao ny <strong>"which"</strong> rehefa <strong>mahafantatra ny safidy</strong> ianao ary mila misafidy amin'izy ireo. Ohatra : misy boky 3 eo anatrehanao, dia hanontany ianao hoe "Which book do you want?"

  <!-- 3. COMPARAISON : WHICH vs WHAT                              -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ Fampitahana : "WHICH" vs "WHAT"</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Tabilao fampitahana :</strong>
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <table class="verb-table">
        <tr><th style="background:#1e40af;">WHICH (voafetra)</th><th style="background:#b45309;">WHAT (tsy voafetra)</th></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> dress should I wear? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(2-3 akanjo fotsiny)</span></td>
        <td><strong class="color-what">What</strong> dress should I wear? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(rehetra mety)</span></td></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> colour do you like? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(lisitra kely)</span></td>
        <td><strong class="color-what">What</strong> colour is it? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(mamantatra loko fotsiny)</span></td></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> student won? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(fantatra ny mpianatra)</span></td>
        <td><strong class="color-what">What</strong> student does that? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(tsy fantatra, hafahafa)</span></td></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-which">Which</strong> day is best? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(7 andro fotsiny)</span></td>
        <td><strong class="color-what">What</strong> day is it today? <br><span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1f3d34;">(mamantatra andro)</span></td></tr>
      </table>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong><br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-which">Which</strong> → "Iza amin'ireo ?" (misy safidy voafetra)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-what">What</strong> → "Inona ?" (tsy misy fetra)
    </div>
  </div>

    <h2>4️⃣ "WHICH" ET "WHAT" EXCLAMATIFS <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Fanehoana fahagagana)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>What</strong> / <strong>Which</strong> + <strong>nom</strong> ! <br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      Enti-maneho <strong>fahagagana</strong>, <strong>fahasosorana</strong>, na <strong>fankasitrahana</strong>.
    </div>

  <!-- 6. ASTUCES POUR LES ÉLÈVES                                  -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🎯 Torohevitra mahasoa ho an'ny mpianatra</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Jereo foana raha <strong>misy safidy voafetra</strong> sa tsia. Raha misy → <strong>which</strong>. Raha tsy misy → <strong>what</strong>.</li>
        <li><strong>Which</strong> dia miaraka amin'ny <strong>anarana (nom)</strong> foana. Tsy mitokana izy.</li>
        <li><strong>What</strong> dia miaraka amin'ny <strong>anarana (nom)</strong> foana ihany koa.</li>
        <li>Ho an'ny <strong>ora</strong> sy <strong>daty</strong>, dia mampiasa <strong>what</strong> foana isika : <em>What time is it?</em></li>
        <li>Ho an'ny <strong>karazana</strong> zavatra iray, dia mampiasa <strong>what</strong> foana isika : <em>What kind of ...?</em></li>
        <li>Ho an'ny <strong>fanehoana fahagagana</strong>, dia mampiasa <strong>what</strong> matetika isika : <em>What a beautiful day!</em></li>
        <li>Tsarovy fa ny <strong>"which"</strong> dia matetika ampiasaina rehefa <strong>misy safidy roa na telo</strong> fotsiny.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Which" = safidy voafetra, "What" = tsy voafetra. Io no fototry ny fampiasana azy roa !
    </div>
  </div>
