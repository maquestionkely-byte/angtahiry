Exercice : Types of Sentences - Niveau 3 (moyen)

Consigne : Identifiez le type de chaque phrase.
A) Declarative / B) Interrogative / C) Imperative / D) Exclamatory

1. It's really a shame you can't attend this ceremony.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tena mampalahelo fa tsy afaka manatrika io lanonana io ianao.

2. Could you tell me where the post office is, please?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana fahalalam-pomba, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Azonao atao ve ny milaza amiko hoe aiza no misy ny paositra?

3. Please wait a moment, your call will be connected.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko fahalalam-pomba, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Miangavy anao hiandry fotoana fohy, ny antso anao dia hifandray.

4. How time flies when you're having fun!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Hafaingana ny fotoana rehefa miala voly!

5. I had never realized the importance of this decision.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara zava-misy, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy mbola nahatsikaritra mihitsy ny maha-zava-dehibe ity fanapahan-kevitra ity aho.

6. Couldn't you try to understand my point of view for once?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy afaka manandrana hahatakatra ny fomba fijeriko ve ianao indray mandeha?

7. Don't open this door before I return, no matter what.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Aza manokatra izany varavarana izany mihitsy alohan'ny hiverenako, na inona na inona mitranga.

8. What a magnificent show we were lucky to see!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Tena fampisehoana lehibe no nahitana anay!

9. Every morning, he gets up at five o'clock to go running.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara zava-misy, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Isaky ny maraina, mifarana amin'ny dimy ora izy mba hihazakazaka.

10. Have you ever thought about all those people who suffer every day?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Efa nieritreritra ny olona rehetra izay mijaly isan'andro ve ianao?
