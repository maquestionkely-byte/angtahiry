  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "relative pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>relative pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> ampiasaina <strong>mampifandray</strong> fehezanteny roa na <strong>manondro</strong> zavatra na olona efa voalaza.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia <strong>mampiditra</strong> ny <strong>relative clause</strong> (fehezanteny mifameno).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"who"</strong> sy <strong>"whom"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>olona</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"which"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>zavatra na biby</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"that"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>olona, zavatra, na biby</strong> (rehetra).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"where"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>toerana</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"whose"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>fananana</strong> (an'ny olona na zavatra).
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "WHO" – Olona <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Sujet – iza)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Who</strong> dia ampiasaina hanondroana <strong>olona</strong> izay <strong>mpanao (sujet)</strong> ny matoanteny.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Iza"</strong> na <strong>"izay"</strong> (olona manao zavatra).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>👤</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">The man <strong class="color-who">who</strong> is here is my father. → Ilay lehilahy <strong>iza</strong> eto dia raiko.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">The girl <strong class="color-who">who</strong> sings is my sister. → Ilay zazavavy <strong>izay</strong> mihira dia rahavaviko.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I know the teacher <strong class="color-who">who</strong> works here. → Fantatro ilay mpampianatra <strong>izay</strong> miasa eto.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">The doctor <strong class="color-who">who</strong> saved her is kind. → Ilay dokotera <strong>izay</strong> namonjy azy dia tsara fanahy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">The woman <strong class="color-who">who</strong> lives next door is friendly. → Ilay vehivavy <strong>izay</strong> mipetraka eo akaiky eo dia sariaka.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
