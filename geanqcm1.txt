1. A male person is called a ___.
A) woman
*B) man
C) girl
D) child
EXPLICATION: "Man" dia ampiasaina ho an'ny olona lahy.
TRADUCTION: Ny olona lahy dia antsoina hoe "man".

2. A female person is called a ___.
*A) woman
B) man
C) boy
D) father
EXPLICATION: "Woman" dia ampiasaina ho an'ny olona vavy.
TRADUCTION: Ny olona vavy dia antsoina hoe "woman".

3. A male child is called a ___.
A) girl
*B) boy
C) woman
D) mother
EXPLICATION: "Boy" dia ampiasaina ho an'ny zaza lahy.
TRADUCTION: Ny zaza lahy dia antsoina hoe "boy".

4. A female child is called a ___.
*A) girl
B) boy
C) man
D) father
EXPLICATION: "Girl" dia ampiasaina ho an'ny zaza vavy.
TRADUCTION: Ny zaza vavy dia antsoina hoe "girl".

5. One book = ___ book.
*A) a
B) an
C) the
D) some
EXPLICATION: "A" dia ampiasaina alohan'ny teny manomboka amin'ny konsonanta (book).
TRADUCTION: Boky iray = "a book".

6. One apple = ___ apple.
A) a
*B) an
C) the
D) some
EXPLICATION: "An" dia ampiasaina alohan'ny teny manomboka amin'ny zanapeo (apple).
TRADUCTION: Paoma iray = "an apple".

7. Two books = ___ books.
A) book
*B) books
C) bookes
D) bookies
EXPLICATION: Ampidirana "s" amin'ny farany ny ankamaroan'ny anarana amin'ny endrika maro.
TRADUCTION: Boky roa = "books".

8. Three boxes = ___ boxes.
A) box
B) boxs
*C) boxes
D) boxies
EXPLICATION: Ampidirana "es" rehefa miafara amin'ny "s", "x", "ch", "sh" ny anarana.
TRADUCTION: Boatiny telo = "boxes".

9. One child = two ___
A) childs
B) childrens
*C) children
D) childes
EXPLICATION: "Child" dia anarana tsy ara-dalàna; ny endrika maro dia "children".
TRADUCTION: Zaza iray = zaza roa ("children").

10. One foot = two ___
A) foots

*C) feet
D) footes
EXPLICATION: "Foot" dia anarana tsy ara-dalàna; ny endrika maro dia "feet".
TRADUCTION: Tongotra iray = tongotra roa ("feet").

*C) teeth
D) toothies
EXPLICATION: "Tooth" dia anarana tsy ara-dalàna; ny endrika maro dia "teeth".

EXPLICATION: "Mouse" dia anarana tsy ara-dalàna; ny endrika maro dia "mice".
TRADUCTION: Voalavo iray = voalavo roa ("mice").
