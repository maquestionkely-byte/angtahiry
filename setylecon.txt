    <h1>SENTENCE TYPES</h1>
    <p style="font-size: 0.9rem; font-weight: 500; margin-top: 4px; color: #1e3a8a;">Karazana fehezanteny amin'ny teny anglisy</p>

  <!-- Carte : Inona ny sentence types ? -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "Sentence Types" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>sentence types</strong> dia ny <strong>karazana fehezanteny</strong> ampiasaina amin'ny fiteny anglisy.<br><br>
      🔹 Misy <strong>karazany efatra (4)</strong> lehibe izy ireo :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Declarative</strong> → Fehezanteny milaza (manambara zavatra)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Interrogative</strong> → Fehezanteny manontany (mametraka fanontaniana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Imperative</strong> → Fehezanteny didy (manome baiko na toromarika)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Exclamatory</strong> → Fehezanteny miantsoantso (mampiseho fihetsehampo mahery)
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 1. DECLARATIVE SENTENCE -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ DECLARATIVE SENTENCE <span class="sentence-tag declarative">Milaza</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>declarative sentence</strong> dia fehezanteny <strong>milaza na manambara zavatra iray</strong>. Izy no karazana fehezanteny ampiasaina indrindra amin'ny resaka andavanandro.
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <strong>Raha fintinina :</strong><br>
      • Manambara zava-misy, hevitra, na fihetsehampo tsotra.<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>point (.)</strong>.<br>
      • Filaharana mahazatra : <strong>Sujet + Verbe + Complément</strong>.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I speak English. (Miteny anglisy aho.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">She lives in Antananarivo. (Mipetraka any Antananarivo izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">We eat rice every day. (Mihinana vary isan'andro izahay.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">He is a teacher. (Mpampianatra izy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Madagascar is a big island. (Nosy lehibe i Madagasikara.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">They like to play football. (Tia milalao baolina kitra izy ireo.)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny declarative no fehezanteny <strong>mahazatra indrindra</strong>. Ampiasaina isan'andro amin'ny resaka sy fanoratana.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 2. INTERROGATIVE SENTENCE -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>2️⃣ INTERROGATIVE SENTENCE <span class="sentence-tag interrogative">Manontany</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>interrogative sentence</strong> dia fehezanteny <strong>mametraka fanontaniana</strong>. Ampiasaina raha te hahafantatra zavatra ianao.
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <strong>Raha fintinina :</strong><br>
      • Mametraka fanontaniana.<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>question mark (?)</strong>.<br>
      • Misy karazany roa : <strong>Yes/No questions</strong> sy <strong>WH-questions</strong>.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Do you speak English? (Miteny anglisy ve ianao?)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Where does she live? (Aiza no misy azy?)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">What time is it? (Amin'ny firy izao?)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Are you hungry? (Noana ve ianao?)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Why do they come here? (Nahoana no tonga atỳ izy ireo?)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Can you help me? (Afaka manampy ahy ve ianao?)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny interrogative dia miafara amin'ny <strong>?</strong>. Misy ihany koa ny fanontaniana amin'ny <strong>question words</strong> : What, Where, When, Why, Who, How.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 3. IMPERATIVE SENTENCE -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ IMPERATIVE SENTENCE <span class="sentence-tag imperative">Didy / Baiko</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>imperative sentence</strong> dia fehezanteny <strong>manome baiko, toromarika, na fangatahana</strong>. Matetika dia tsy misy <strong>sujet</strong> (you) fa tsy voasoratra nefa takatra.
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <strong>Raha fintinina :</strong><br>
      • Manome baiko, toromarika, na fangatahana.<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>point (.)</strong> na <strong>exclamation point (!)</strong>.<br>
      • Ny matoanteny dia amin'ny endrika <strong>base form</strong> (tsy misy -s na -ed).
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Open the door. (Sokafy ny varavarana.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Sit down, please. (Mipetraha, azafady.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Don't speak loudly. (Aza miteny mafy.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Listen to me! (Henoy aho!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Write your name here. (Soraty eto ny anaranao.)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Be careful! (Tandremo!)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny imperative dia mampiasa ny matoanteny <strong>tsy misy fiovana</strong>. Raha fandavana dia ampiasao ny <strong>"Don't"</strong> + verbe.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- 4. EXCLAMATORY SENTENCE -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>4️⃣ EXCLAMATORY SENTENCE <span class="sentence-tag exclamatory">Miantsoantso</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Famaritana malagasy :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>exclamatory sentence</strong> dia fehezanteny <strong>mampiseho fihetsehampo na hery</strong> toy ny fahagagana, fahatezerana, fifaliana, na tahotra.
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <strong>Raha fintinina :</strong><br>
      • Mampiseho fihetsehampo mahery vaika.<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>exclamation point (!)</strong>.<br>
      • Matetika manomboka amin'ny <strong>"What"</strong> na <strong>"How"</strong>.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>✨</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">What a beautiful day! (Tena andro tsara izao!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">How wonderful! (Tena mahafinaritra!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">That's amazing! (Tena mahagaga izany!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">What a big house! (Trano lehibe tokoa!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">How fast he runs! (Tena haingana izy!)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I can't believe it! (Tsy mino aho!)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny exclamatory dia miafara amin'ny <strong>!</strong>. Mampiasa ny <strong>"What"</strong> ho an'ny anarana ary <strong>"How"</strong> ho an'ny adjectif na adverbe.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ==================== TABLEAU RÉCAPITULATIF ==================== -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📊 Tabilao famintinana (Tableau récapitulatif)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>Declarative :</strong><br>
      • Manambara zavatra<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>.</strong><br>
      • Ohatra : <em>I like coffee.</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Interrogative :</strong><br>
      • Mametraka fanontaniana<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>?</strong><br>
      • Ohatra : <em>Do you like coffee?</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Imperative :</strong><br>
      • Manome baiko na toromarika<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>.</strong> na <strong>!</strong><br>
      • Ohatra : <em>Drink your coffee!</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Exclamatory :</strong><br>
      • Mampiseho fihetsehampo mahery<br>
      • Mamarana amin'ny <strong>!</strong><br>
      • Ohatra : <em>What good coffee!</em>
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern" style="margin-top: 12px;">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🧪</span> Ohatra fampitahana ny karazana fehezanteny rehetra
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">You are happy. (Declarative)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Are you happy? (Interrogative)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Be happy! (Imperative)</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">What a happy day! (Exclamatory)</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ==================== FANAZAVANINA FANAMPINY ==================== -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fahasamihafana sy fanampim-panazavana</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Inona no maha-samihafa azy ireo?</strong><br><br>
      <strong>Declarative</strong> → <em>Milaza zavatra tsotra.</em><br>
      Ohatra : <em>She is a nurse.</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Interrogative</strong> → <em>Mametraka fanontaniana.</em><br>
      Ohatra : <em>Is she a nurse?</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Imperative</strong> → <em>Manome baiko.</em><br>
      Ohatra : <em>Call the nurse!</em><br><br>
      
      <strong>Exclamatory</strong> → <em>Mampiseho fihetsehampo.</em><br>
      Ohatra : <em>What a good nurse!</em>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      🧠 <strong>Fomba tsarovana mora :</strong><br>
      - Raha <strong>milaza</strong> → Declarative (.)<br>
      - Raha <strong>mametra-panontaniana</strong> → Interrogative (?)<br>
      - Raha <strong>manome baiko</strong> → Imperative (.) na (!)<br>
      - Raha <strong>miantsoantso</strong> → Exclamatory (!)
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Carte Torohevitra -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🎯 Torohevitra mahasoa ho an'ny débutant</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Jereo foana ny <strong>famaranana</strong> (., ?, !) mba hahalala ny karazan'ny fehezanteny.</li>
        <li>Mba hahalalana ny <strong>interrogative</strong>, jereo raha misy "Do/Does/Did/Is/Are/Can/Will" eo alohan'ny sujet.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>imperative</strong> dia miala amin'ny "you" nefa takatra ho "you".</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>exclamatory</strong> matetika manomboka amin'ny "What" na "How".</li>
        <li>Vakio mafy ny ohatra isan'andro mba hianarana ny feon'ny karazana tsirairay.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny fianarana karazana fehezanteny dia manampy anao hahay <strong>miresaka sy manoratra tsara kokoa</strong> amin'ny teny anglisy. Mianara tsikelikely ary aza matahotra diso !
    </div>
  </div>

    function detectDevTools() {
      let devToolsOpen = false;
      const threshold = 160;
      setInterval(() => {
        const widthDiff = window.outerWidth - window.innerWidth;
        const heightDiff = window.outerHeight - window.innerHeight;
        if (widthDiff > threshold || heightDiff > threshold) {
          if (!devToolsOpen) { devToolsOpen = true; showBlockOverlay(); }
        } else {
          if (devToolsOpen) { devToolsOpen = false; hideBlockOverlay(); }
        }
      }, 1000);
    }
