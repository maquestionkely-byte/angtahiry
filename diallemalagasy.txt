titre : Fahatsiraovana

HERY : I was thinking back to our first date… Do you remember the little café under the jacarandas? 🌿

Traduction : Nieritreritra ny fitsidihana voalohany nataontsika aho… Tsaroanao ve ilay kafe kely teo ambanin'ny jacaranda? 🌿

FANIRY : You had brought a rose and the fine rain surprised us, we laughed like children! ❤️

Traduction : Nitondra raozy ianao ary nahagaga antsika ny orana madinika, nihomehy toy ny ankizy isika! ❤️

HERY : That day, I knew you were a spark in my life. Even the rain was magical with you ✨

Traduction : Tamin'io andro io, dia fantatro fa ianao no tselatra kely namelona ny fiainako. Na ny orana aza dia nahagaga raha niaraka taminao ✨

FANIRY : (｡♡‿♡｡) Every moment with you is a memory I keep warm. Thank you for being my happiness, Hery.

Traduction : Ny fotoana rehetra niarahanay dia fahatsiarovana tiako ho voatahiry mafana. Misaotra anao fa ianao no fahasambarako, Hery.

HERY : And that scent of rain and coffee mixed with your laughter… I will never forget it. My heart was already yours. And I also remember the way you burst out laughing when the drop of water fell on your nose. Wonderful.

Traduction : Ary io fofon'ny orana sy ny kafe mifangaro amin'ny fihomehezanao… Tsy hohadinoiko mihitsy. Efa anao ny foko. Ary tsaroko ihany koa ny fomba nihomehezanao mafy rehefa nilatsaka teo amin'ny oronao ny rano indray mitete. Mahafinaritra.

FANIRY : Hery… that day changed my life. With you, every memory is a star in my sky. I love you. 🌟

Traduction : Hery… nanova ny fiainako io andro io. Raha miaraka aminao, ny fahatsiarovana rehetra dia kintana eny amin'ny lanitro. Tiako ianao. 🌟
