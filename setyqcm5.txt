Exercice : Types of Sentences - Niveau 5 (très difficile)

Consigne : Identifiez le type de chaque phrase.
A) Declarative / B) Interrogative / C) Imperative / D) Exclamatory

1. Perhaps we should reconsider our position in the current circumstances.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Angamba mety, amin'ny toe-javatra misy ankehitriny, ny mandinika indray ny toerantsika.

2. Will you finally stop complaining about trivial things?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Hijanona ny fitarainana amin'ny zavatra madinika ve ianao farany?

3. Let him who is without sin cast the first stone.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Avelao izay tsy manota hanipy vato voalohany.

4. How sweet and soothing it is to feel the sea breeze caress your face at dawn!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Mankarary ny fihetsehan'ny rivotra an-dranomasina mikitika ny tavany amin'ny maraina!

5. I cannot believe that you were completely unaware of this crucial information.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy afaka mino aho fa tsy fantatrao tanteraka izany vaovao manan-danja izany.

6. Would it really be the last time we see each other?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Tena ity ve no farany hahitantsika?

7. Know that I will not tolerate any misconduct during your internship.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Fantaro fa tsy hanakana ny fitondran-tena ratsy aho mandritra ny fiofananao.

8. What wonderful childhood memories this song brings back to me!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Mampahatsiahy ahy ny fahatsiarovana mahafinaritra tamin'ny fahazazako ity hira ity!

9. No one had predicted that the accident would happen at that precise moment.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara zava-misy, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy nisy naminavina fa hitranga amin'izany fotoana izany ny loza.

10. Couldn't you show a little more discernment?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy afaka manao fahiratan-tsaina kely bebe kokoa ve ianao?
