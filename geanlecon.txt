  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "Gender and Number" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>Gender</strong> (lahy na vavy) dia mamaritra raha <strong>lahy (masculine)</strong> na <strong>vavy (feminine)</strong> ny anarana na mpisolo anarana.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>Number</strong> (isa) dia mamaritra raha <strong>tokana (singular)</strong> na <strong>maro (plural)</strong> ny anarana na mpisolo anarana.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Masculine</strong> → anarana lahy (ohatra: boy, man, he, his)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Feminine</strong> → anarana vavy (ohatra: girl, woman, she, hers)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Singular</strong> → zavatra iray ihany (ohatra: book, one, this)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Plural</strong> → zavatra mihoatra ny iray (ohatra: books, many, these)<br><br>
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe :</strong> Amin'ny teny anglisy, ny <strong>anarana</strong> dia tsy manana maha-lahy na vavy (neutral) fa ny <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronouns)</strong> sy ny <strong>mpamaritra (possessives)</strong> no manao izany.
    </div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>

      <strong>📌 Mpisolo anarana (Pronouns) :</strong><br>
      <strong>he, him, his, himself</strong><br><br>
      <strong>📌 Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy :</strong><br>
      • <strong>He</strong> is my brother.<br>
      • I saw <strong>him</strong>.<br>
      • This is <strong>his</strong> book.
    </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">

      • <strong>Singular</strong> → anarana tsy misy "s" farany (ohatra : <strong>book</strong>, <strong>car</strong>, <strong>house</strong>)<br>
      • <strong>Plural</strong> → anarana misy "s" na "es" farany (ohatra : <strong>books</strong>, <strong>cars</strong>, <strong>houses</strong>)

          <li><strong>woman</strong> → vehivavy (iray)</li>
        </ul>

      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Jereo foana raha <strong>lahy (masculine)</strong> na <strong>vavy (feminine)</strong> ilay olona resahina.</li>
        <li>Jereo foana raha <strong>tokana (singular)</strong> na <strong>maro (plural)</strong> ilay zavatra resahina.</li>
        <li>Amin'ny teny anglisy, ny <strong>anarana</strong> dia tsy manana maha-lahy na vavy.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronouns)</strong> no mampiseho ny maha-lahy na vavy : <strong>he, she, it, they</strong>.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>singular</strong> dia ho an'ny zavatra iray ihany : <strong>this, that, it, he, she</strong>.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>plural</strong> dia ho an'ny zavatra maro : <strong>these, those, they, we</strong>.</li>
        <li>Ny anarana maro amin'ny teny anglisy dia mifarana amin'ny <strong>"s"</strong> na <strong>"es"</strong>.</li>
