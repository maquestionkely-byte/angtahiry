  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "demonstrative pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>demonstrative pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> ampiasaina <strong>hanondroana zavatra na olona</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia <strong>mijoro irery</strong> (tsy miaraka amin'ny anarana).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"this one"</strong> sy <strong>"that one"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>zavatra tokana (singulier)</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"these"</strong> sy <strong>"those"</strong> dia ho an'ny <strong>zavatra maro (pluriel)</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>This one</strong> → zavatra <strong>akaiky</strong> (tokana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>That one</strong> → zavatra <strong>lavitra</strong> (tokana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>These</strong> → zavatra maro <strong>akaiky</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Those</strong> → zavatra maro <strong>lavitra</strong>
    </div>
  </div>

      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      <strong>Déterminant (mpamaritra)</strong> → miaraka amin'ny <strong>anarana (nom)</strong><br>
      <strong>Pronom (mpisolo)</strong> → <strong>mijoro irery</strong> (tsy misy anarana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Déterminant :</strong> This book is mine. (Miaraka amin'ny "book")<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</strong> <strong>Pronom :</strong> This one is mine. (Mijoro irery)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Déterminant :</strong> These shoes are new. (Miaraka amin'ny "shoes")<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Pronom :</strong> These are new. (Mijoro irery)
    </div>

      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong>Déterminant :</strong> <strong class="color-these">These</strong> shoes are new. → <strong>Ireto</strong> kiraro ireto no vaovao.</span>
