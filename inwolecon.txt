    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "Interrogative Words" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>interrogative words</strong> dia <strong>teny fampiasaina hanontaniana</strong> mba hahazoana fampahalalana.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia apetraka <strong>eo am-piandohan'ny fehezanteny</strong> (na eo alohan'ny matoanteny).<br><br>
      🔹 Antsoina koa hoe <strong>"Wh-words"</strong> satria ny ankamaroany dia manomboka amin'ny "wh" (why, what, when, where) — fa misy koa "how" sy "how many".<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Why</strong> → manontany <strong>antony</strong> (Nahoana?)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>How</strong> → manontany <strong>fomba</strong> (Ahoana?)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>What</strong> → manontany <strong>zavatra</strong> (Inona?)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>When</strong> → manontany <strong>fotoana</strong> (Rahoviana?)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Where</strong> → manontany <strong>toerana</strong> (Aiza?)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>How many</strong> → manontany <strong>isa</strong> (Firy?)
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "WHY" – Nahoana? <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Antony / Reason)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Why</strong> dia ampiasaina hanontaniana ny <strong>antony</strong> na ny <strong>fototra</strong> nahatonga zavatra iray.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Valiny :</strong> matetika misy "because" (satria).
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>❓</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> are you sad? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no malahelo ianao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> did you come late? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no tara ianao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> is she crying? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no mitomany izy?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> do you study English? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no mianatra teny anglisy ianao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> not? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no tsia?</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Why" dia manontany ny <strong>antony</strong>. Ny valiny matetika dia manomboka amin'ny "because".</div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>How</strong> dia ampiasaina hanontaniana ny <strong>fomba</strong> na ny <strong>toetran'ny</strong> zavatra iray.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Valiny :</strong> mamaritra ny fomba na toetra.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>❓</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-how">How</strong> are you? → <strong class="color-how">Ahoana</strong> ianao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-how">How</strong> do you go to school? → <strong class="color-how">Ahoana</strong> no andehananao any am-pianarana?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-how">How</strong> does it work? → <strong class="color-how">Ahoana</strong> no fiasany?</span></div>

      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-how">How</strong> can I help you? → <strong class="color-how">Ahoana</strong> no ahafahako manampy anao?</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "How" dia manontany ny <strong>fomba</strong> na ny <strong>toetra</strong>. Ampiasaina koa izy hilazana <strong>habetsahana</strong> miaraka amin'ny adjectives (how tall, how big).</div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>What</strong> dia ampiasaina hanontaniana ny <strong>zavatra</strong>, ny <strong>asa</strong>, na ny <strong>fanazavana</strong> momba zavatra iray.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Valiny :</strong> anarana na fanazavana momba ilay zavatra.

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>How many</strong> dia ampiasaina hanontaniana ny <strong>isa</strong> na ny <strong>habetsahan'ny</strong> zavatra azo isaina.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Valiny :</strong> isa (1, 2, 3, ...).<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> Ampiasaina amin'ny <strong>zavatra azo isaina</strong> (countable nouns) ihany.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>❓</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>

      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-howmany">How many</strong> apples do you want? → <strong class="color-howmany">Firy</strong> ny paoma tadiavinao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-howmany">How many</strong> students are in your class? → <strong class="color-howmany">Firy</strong> ny mpianatra ao amin'ny kilasinao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-howmany">How many</strong> times did you go? → <strong class="color-howmany">Firy</strong> indray ianao no nandeha?</span></div>
    </div>

    </div>

      ✅ <strong class="color-why">Why</strong> → nahoana (antony)<br>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Structure amin'ny fanontaniana</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Ny firafitry ny fanontaniana amin'ny interrogative words :</strong><br><br>
      <strong>Interrogative Word + Auxiliaire / Verbe + Sujet + Verbe principal + ... ?</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • <strong>What</strong> are you doing?<br>
      • <strong>Where</strong> do you live?<br>
      • <strong>Why</strong> is she crying?<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> Raha "who" no ampiasaina ho <strong>sujet</strong>, dia tsy misy auxiliaire fanampiny (ohatra : Who called you?).

      <div class="exo-titre"><span>📝</span> Firafitry ny fanontaniana</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-what">What</strong> + are + you + doing? → <strong class="color-what">Inona</strong> no ataonao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-where">Where</strong> + do + you + live? → <strong class="color-where">Aiza</strong> no ipetrahanao?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-why">Why</strong> + did + she + go? → <strong class="color-why">Nahoana</strong> no nandeha izy?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-how">How</strong> + do + you + spell + this? → <strong class="color-how">Ahoana</strong> no fanoratanao ity?</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-when">When</strong> + will + they + come? → <strong class="color-when">Rahoviana</strong> no ho avy izy ireo?</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny interrogative words dia apetraka <strong>eo am-piandohana</strong> ny fehezanteny. Avy eo ny <strong>auxiliaire</strong> (do, does, did, is, are, was, were, will...), arahan'ny <strong>sujet</strong>, ary ny <strong>verbe principal</strong>.</div>
