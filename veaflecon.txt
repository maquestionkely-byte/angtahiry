  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- Carte : Inona ny verbs after prepositions ?                 -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "verbs after prepositions" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Rehefa misy <strong>mpampiankin-teny (preposition)</strong> toy ny <strong>"to"</strong>, <strong>"of"</strong>, ary <strong>"for"</strong>, dia ny matoanteny manaraka azy dia <strong>ao amin'ny endrika infinitif</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>infinitif</strong> dia ny endrika fototry ny matoanteny miaraka amin'ny <strong>"to"</strong> (to + verbe).<br><br>
      🔹 Rehefa misy <strong>"to"</strong> ho mpampiankin-teny (tsy ho an'ny infinitif), dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> ihany no arahiny.<br><br>
      🔹 Rehefa misy <strong>"of"</strong> na <strong>"for"</strong> ho mpampiankin-teny, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> koa no arahiny.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Règle générale :</strong><br>
      <strong>Préposition (to / of / for) + VERBE (infinitif)</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • I want <strong>to go</strong>. (to + go)<br>
      • She is afraid <strong>of to walk</strong> → She is afraid <strong>to walk</strong>. (of + to walk)<br>
      • This is for <strong>to help</strong> → This is <strong>to help</strong>. (for + to help)<br><br>
      <div style="background:#fef1cf;padding:10px;border-radius:16px;margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Zava-dehibe :</strong> Rehefa misy mpampiankin-teny toy ny <strong>"to"</strong>, <strong>"of"</strong>, na <strong>"for"</strong>, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> (infinitif) no manaraka azy.
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 1. VERBES APRÈS "TO" (PRÉPOSITION)                        -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ Verbes après "TO" <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Préposition – to + infinitif)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      Rehefa misy <strong>"to"</strong> ho <strong>mpampiankin-teny</strong> (fa tsy mba ao amin'ny infinitif), dia <strong>"to + infinitif"</strong> no manaraka azy.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      "To" + matoanteny amin'ny endrika fototry (infinitif).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🔗</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>

      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">

        <span class="fr">He wants <strong class="color-to">to play</strong>. → Te-hilalao izy. (to + play)</span>

    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Rehefa misy <strong>"to"</strong> (mpampiankin-teny), dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> (infinitif) no arahiny. Ohatra : <em>I want <strong>to go</strong>.</em>

  <!-- 5. DIFFÉRENCE : INFINITIF vs PRÉPOSITION + INFINITIF      -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fahasamihafana : Infinitif vs Préposition + Infinitif</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Infinitif</strong> → <strong>to + verbe</strong> (ohatra : to eat, to go)<br>
      Ohatra : <em>I want <strong>to eat</strong>.</em> (Te-hihinana aho.)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Préposition + Infinitif</strong> → <strong>to + to + verbe</strong><br>
      Ohatra : <em>She is afraid <strong>of to walk</strong>.</em> → <em>She is afraid <strong>to walk</strong>.</em><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Fanamarihana :</strong> Amin'ny teny anglisy, rehefa misy mpampiankin-teny toy ny <strong>"of"</strong> sy <strong>"for"</strong>, dia matetika <strong>esorina</strong> ny "to" faharoa, ka lasa <strong>to + verbe</strong> fotsiny.<br><br>
      <div style="background:#fef1cf;padding:10px;border-radius:16px;margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny mpampiankin-teny (to, of, for) dia <strong>mampiditra</strong> ny infinitif. Amin'ny fampiasana andavanandro, dia <strong>"to + verbe"</strong> ihany no hita.
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Rehefa misy <strong>mpampiankin-teny (to, of, for)</strong>, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> (infinitif) no manaraka azy.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>infinitif</strong> dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> (ohatra : to go, to eat, to sing).</li>
        <li>Rehefa misy <strong>"to"</strong>, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> fotsiny.</li>
        <li>Rehefa misy <strong>"of"</strong> na <strong>"for"</strong>, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> koa no arahiny.</li>
        <li>Tsarovy fa ny <strong>to</strong> dia mety ho <strong>mpampiankin-teny</strong> na <strong>mpamaritra ny infinitif</strong>.</li>
        <li>Amin'ny fampiasana andavanandro, dia <strong>to + verbe</strong> ihany no ampiasaina.</li>
        <li>Jereo tsara ny <strong>mpampiankin-teny</strong> alohan'ny hamenoanao ny banga.</li>
        <li>Zahao foana ny <strong>endrika tsy miova</strong> (base form) ilay verbe.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "To" + verbe, "Of" + to + verbe, "For" + to + verbe. Ny zava-dehibe dia ny <strong>to + verbe</strong> (infinitif) no manaraka ny mpampiankin-teny.

    function detectDevTools() {
      let devToolsOpen = false;
      const threshold = 160;
      setInterval(() => {
        const widthDiff = window.outerWidth - window.innerWidth;

        if (widthDiff > threshold || heightDiff > threshold) {
