1. The committee, along with its advisors, ___ meeting today.
*A) is
B) are
C) am
D) be
EXPLICATION: "Along with" tsy manova ny zana-pehezanteny; "committee" dia singulier, ka "is".
TRADUCTION: Ny komity, miaraka amin'ny mpanolotsainy, dia manao fivoriana androany.

2. Neither the manager nor his employees ___ satisfied with the outcome.
A) is
*B) are
C) am
D) be
EXPLICATION: "Neither...nor" mifanaraka amin'ny akaiky indrindra (employees = they = are).
TRADUCTION: Na ny mpitantana na ny mpiasa dia tsy afa-po amin'ny vokatra.

3. All of the evidence ___ pointing to the same conclusion.
*A) is
B) are
C) am
D) be

TRADUCTION: Ny porofo rehetra dia manondro ny fehin-kevitra iray ihany.

*B) have
