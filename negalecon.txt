      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Not</strong> → tsia / tsy (ilay fototry ny fandavana)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>No longer</strong> → tsy...intsony (tsy mitohy intsony)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Never</strong> → tsy...mihitsy / tsy mbola (tsy misy fotoana)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Neither...nor</strong> → na...na (fandavana roa sosona)<br>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "NOT" – Tsy / Tsia <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Fandavana fototra)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Not</strong> dia ampiasaina <strong>handàna</strong> zavatra iray. Izy no fototry ny negation.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Miaraka amin'ny auxiliaire :</strong> ampidirina aorian'ny auxiliaire (do, does, did, is, are, was, were, will, can, etc.)<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Misy endrika fohy (contractions) :</strong> don't, doesn't, didn't, isn't, aren't, wasn't, weren't, won't, can't, etc.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>🚫</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I do <strong class="color-not">not</strong> like coffee. → <strong class="color-not">Tsy</strong> tia kafe aho.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">She is <strong class="color-not">not</strong> here. → <strong class="color-not">Tsy</strong> eto izy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">They did <strong class="color-not">not</strong> come. → <strong class="color-not">Tsy</strong> tonga izy ireo.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I can <strong class="color-not">not</strong> swim. → <strong class="color-not">Tsy</strong> mahay milomano aho.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">He will <strong class="color-not">not</strong> go. → <strong class="color-not">Tsy</strong> handeha izy.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Not" dia ampidirina <strong>aorian'ny auxiliaire</strong>. Misy endrika fohy toy ny <strong>don't, doesn't, isn't, can't, won't</strong>.</div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>

      <strong>📌 Toerana :</strong> apetraka <strong>eo alohan'ny matoanteny</strong> na <strong>aorian'ny auxiliaire</strong>.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>⏹️</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> live here. → <strong class="color-nolonger">Tsy</strong> mipetraka <strong class="color-nolonger">intsony</strong> eto aho.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">She is <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> a student. → <strong class="color-nolonger">Tsy</strong> mpianatra <strong class="color-nolonger">intsony</strong> izy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">They <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> work here. → <strong class="color-nolonger">Tsy</strong> miasa <strong class="color-nolonger">intsony</strong> eto izy ireo.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">He <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> smokes. → <strong class="color-nolonger">Tsy</strong> mifoka sigara <strong class="color-nolonger">intsony</strong> izy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">We are <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> friends. → <strong class="color-nolonger">Tsy</strong> namana <strong class="color-nolonger">intsony</strong> izahay.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "No longer" dia ampiasaina rehefa <strong>tsy mitohy intsony</strong> zavatra iray izay efa nisy taloha.</div>
  </div>

  <!-- 3. NEVER – Tsy...mihitsy / Tsy mbola                       -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ "NEVER" – Tsy...mihitsy / Tsy mbola <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#0d9488;font-weight:400;">(Tsy misy fotoana)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Never</strong> dia ampiasaina hilazana fa <strong>tsy nisy mihitsy</strong> na <strong>tsy mbola</strong> nitranga zavatra iray.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Toerana :</strong> apetraka <strong>eo alohan'ny matoanteny</strong> na <strong>aorian'ny auxiliaire</strong>.<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> "Never" dia <strong>tsy mila "not"</strong> intsony satria efa manana dikany mandà izy.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>❌</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I have <strong class="color-never">never</strong> been to Paris. → <strong class="color-never">Tsy mbola</strong> nankany Paris aho.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">She <strong class="color-never">never</strong> eats meat. → <strong class="color-never">Tsy</strong> mihinana hena <strong class="color-never">mihitsy</strong> izy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">They will <strong class="color-never">never</strong> know. → <strong class="color-never">Tsy</strong> hahalala <strong class="color-never">mihitsy</strong> izy ireo.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I <strong class="color-never">never</strong> lie. → <strong class="color-never">Tsy</strong> mandainga <strong class="color-never">mihitsy</strong> aho.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">He has <strong class="color-never">never</strong> seen snow. → <strong class="color-never">Tsy mbola</strong> nahita oram-panala mihitsy izy.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Never" dia midika hoe <strong>"tsy...mihitsy"</strong> na <strong>"tsy mbola"</strong>. Tsy mila "not" intsony izy.</div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 4. NEITHER...NOR – Na...na (fandavana roa sosona)           -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>4️⃣ "NEITHER...NOR" – Na...na (fandavana roa sosona) <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#7c3aed;font-weight:400;">(Fandavana roa)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Neither...nor</strong> dia ampiasaina <strong>handàna zavatra roa</strong> miaraka. Midika hoe "tsy izy roa" na "na izy na izy".<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Structure :</strong> <strong>Neither + A + nor + B</strong> (A sy B dia zavatra roa lavina).<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> Ny matoanteny dia mifanaraka amin'ny <strong>B</strong> (ilay akaiky indrindra amin'ny matoanteny).
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>⚖️</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-neither">Neither</strong> John <strong class="color-neither">nor</strong> Mary is here. → <strong class="color-neither">Na</strong> John <strong class="color-neither">na</strong> Mary dia tsy eto.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I like <strong class="color-neither">neither</strong> coffee <strong class="color-neither">nor</strong> tea. → <strong class="color-neither">Na</strong> kafe <strong class="color-neither">na</strong> dite dia tsy tiako.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">She speaks <strong class="color-neither">neither</strong> English <strong class="color-neither">nor</strong> French. → <strong class="color-neither">Na</strong> anglisy <strong class="color-neither">na</strong> frantsay dia tsy miteny izy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-neither">Neither</strong> my brother <strong class="color-neither">nor</strong> my sister came. → <strong class="color-neither">Na</strong> ny rahalahiko <strong class="color-neither">na</strong> ny rahavaviko dia tsy tonga.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">I have <strong class="color-neither">neither</strong> money <strong class="color-neither">nor</strong> time. → <strong class="color-neither">Na</strong> vola <strong class="color-neither">na</strong> fotoana dia tsy manana aho.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Neither...nor" dia ampiasaina <strong>handàna zavatra roa</strong>. Ny matoanteny dia mifanaraka amin'ny <strong>B</strong> (ilavy akaiky indrindra).</div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 5. NOBODY – Tsy misy olona                                 -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>5️⃣ "NOBODY" – Tsy misy olona <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#d97706;font-weight:400;">(Tsy misy olona)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>Nobody</strong> dia midika hoe <strong>"tsy misy olona"</strong>. Ampiasaina hilazana fa tsy nisy olona nanao zavatra iray.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Toerana :</strong> matetika apetraka <strong>eo am-piandohana</strong> ny fehezanteny, ary ny matoanteny dia <strong>singular</strong>.<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> "Nobody" dia <strong>tsy mila "not"</strong> intsony satria efa manana dikany mandà izy.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>👤</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-nobody">Nobody</strong> knows the answer. → <strong class="color-nobody">Tsy misy olona</strong> mahalala ny valiny.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-nobody">Nobody</strong> came to the party. → <strong class="color-nobody">Tsy nisy olona</strong> tonga tamin'ny fety.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-nobody">Nobody</strong> saw me. → <strong class="color-nobody">Tsy nisy olona</strong> nahita ahy.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-nobody">Nobody</strong> is perfect. → <strong class="color-nobody">Tsy misy olona</strong> tonga lafatra.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr"><strong class="color-nobody">Nobody</strong> told me. → <strong class="color-nobody">Tsy nisy olona</strong> nilaza tamiko.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Nobody" dia midika hoe <strong>"tsy misy olona"</strong>. Tsy mila "not" intsony izy.</div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>6️⃣ "NOTHING" – Tsy misy zavatra <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#dc2626;font-weight:400;">(Tsy misy zavatra)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>

      <strong>📌 Toerana :</strong> matetika apetraka <strong>eo am-piandohana</strong> ny fehezanteny, ary ny matoanteny dia <strong>singular</strong>.<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> "Nothing" dia <strong>tsy mila "not"</strong> intsony satria efa manana dikany mandà izy.

  <div class="card">
    <h2>📊 Tabilao famintinana (Tableau récapitulatif)</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Fampitahana ny negation :</strong>
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <table class="verb-table">
        <tr><th style="background:#1e40af;">Negation</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Dikany</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Fampiasana</th></tr>

      ✅ <strong class="color-not">not</strong> → tsy (fandavana fototra)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-nolonger">no longer</strong> → tsy...intsony (tsy mitohy)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-never">never</strong> → tsy...mihitsy (tsy misy fotoana)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-neither">neither...nor</strong> → na...na (fandavana roa)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-nobody">nobody</strong> → tsy misy olona<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-nothing">nothing</strong> → tsy misy zavatra

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fanazavana momba ny "Double Negation"</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      Amin'ny teny anglisy, ny <strong>double negation</strong> (fandavana roa sosona) dia <strong>tsy azo ampiasaina</strong> amin'ny fomba ofisialy.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> ❌ <strong>Diso :</strong> I <strong>don't</strong> have <strong>nothing</strong>.<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> ✅ <strong>Marina :</strong> I <strong>don't</strong> have <strong>anything</strong>. / I have <strong>nothing</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> ❌ <strong>Diso :</strong> <strong>Nobody</strong> <strong>didn't</strong> come.<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> ✅ <strong>Marina :</strong> <strong>Nobody</strong> came. / <strong>Everybody</strong> came.<br><br>
      <strong>⚠️ Tsarovy :</strong> Ny teny toy ny <strong>nobody, nothing, never, neither</strong> dia <strong>efa mandà</strong> ka tsy mila "not" intsony.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre"><span>⚠️</span> Ohatra mampiseho ny double negation diso sy marina</div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">❌ I don't know <strong>nothing</strong>. → ✅ I don't know <strong>anything</strong>. / I know <strong>nothing</strong>.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">❌ She doesn't like <strong>nobody</strong>. → ✅ She doesn't like <strong>anybody</strong>. / She likes <strong>nobody</strong>.</span></div>
      <div class="exo-ligne"><span class="fr">❌ He never goes <strong>nowhere</strong>. → ✅ He never goes <strong>anywhere</strong>. / He goes <strong>nowhere</strong>.</span></div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Amin'ny teny anglisy, <strong>tsy azo atao</strong> ny mampiasa "not" miaraka amin'ny <strong>nobody, nothing, never, nowhere, neither</strong>. Mampiasà "any" (anything, anybody, anywhere) rehefa misy "not".</div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>🎯 Torohevitra mahasoa ho an'ny mpianatra</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"not"</strong> ho an'ny <strong>fandavana fototra</strong> (miaraka amin'ny auxiliaire).</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"no longer"</strong> rehefa <strong>tsy mitohy intsony</strong> zavatra iray.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"never"</strong> rehefa <strong>tsy nisy mihitsy</strong> na <strong>tsy mbola</strong> nitranga.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"neither...nor"</strong> rehefa <strong>handà zavatra roa</strong> miaraka.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"nobody"</strong> rehefa <strong>tsy misy olona</strong>.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"nothing"</strong> rehefa <strong>tsy misy zavatra</strong>.</li>
        <li><strong>Aza</strong> mampiasa "not" miaraka amin'ny <strong>nobody, nothing, never, neither</strong> (double negation).</li>
        <li>Rehefa misy "not" dia mampiasà <strong>anything</strong> fa tsy "nothing", ary <strong>anybody</strong> fa tsy "nobody".</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>neither...nor</strong> dia mitovy amin'ny <strong>not...either</strong>.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>never</strong> dia matetika ampiasaina amin'ny <strong>present perfect</strong> (have/has + ever).</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Not" = tsy (fandavana fototra). "Never" = tsy...mihitsy (tsy misy fotoana). "Nobody" = tsy misy olona. "Nothing" = tsy misy zavatra. Ary <strong>aza</strong> mampiasa "not" miaraka amin'izy ireo (double negation) !
    </div>
  </div>
