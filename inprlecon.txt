  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "interrogative pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>interrogative pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> ampiasaina <strong>hanontaniana</strong> momba ny <strong>olona</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia mamela antsika <strong>hanontany</strong> amin'ny fomba <strong>mazava sy marina</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"who"</strong> dia ampiasaina ho an'ny <strong>sujet</strong> (mpanao).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"whom"</strong> dia ampiasaina ho an'ny <strong>objet</strong> (iharan'ny atao).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"to whom"</strong>, <strong>"of whom"</strong>, <strong>"for whom"</strong> dia ampiasaina rehefa misy <strong>mpampiankin-teny (préposition)</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Who</strong> → Iza no (manao)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Whom</strong> → Iza no (iharan'ny atao)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>To whom</strong> → Ho an'iza / Amin'iza<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Of whom</strong> → An'iza / Iza no manana<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>For whom</strong> → Ho an'iza
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fahasamihafana : WHO vs WHOM</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>WHO</strong> → ampiasaina rehefa <strong>ilay olona no manao</strong> ny asa (sujet).<br>
      Ohatra : <em><strong>Who</strong> called you?</em> → <strong>Iza no</strong> niantso anao? (ilay niantso no manao)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>WHOM</strong> → ampiasaina rehefa <strong>ilay olona no iharan'ny atao</strong> (objet).<br>
      Ohatra : <em><strong>Whom</strong> did you see?</em> → <strong>Iza no</strong> nahitanao? (ilay olona no iharan'ny fahitana)<br><br>
      <div style="background:#fef1cf;padding:10px;border-radius:16px;margin-top:8px;">

  <div class="card">
    <h2>🎯 Torohevitra mahasoa ho an'ny mpianatra</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Jereo raha <strong>mpanao (sujet)</strong> sa <strong>iharan'ny atao (objet)</strong> ilay olona resahina.</li>
        <li>Raha <strong>mpanao</strong> → <strong>"who"</strong> no ampiasaina.</li>
        <li>Raha <strong>iharan'ny atao</strong> → <strong>"whom"</strong> no ampiasaina.</li>
        <li>Rehefa misy <strong>mpampiankin-teny (to, of, for)</strong>, dia ampiasao ny <strong>"to whom"</strong>, <strong>"of whom"</strong>, na <strong>"for whom"</strong>.</li>
        <li>Amin'ny resaka andavanandro, ny <strong>"who"</strong> no ampiasaina matetika kokoa.</li>
        <li>Ny <strong>"whom"</strong> sy ny endrika miaraka amin'ny mpampiankin-teny dia ampiasaina amin'ny fomba ofisialy na an-tsoratra.</li>
        <li>Mba hahafantarana raha <strong>"who"</strong> sa <strong>"whom"</strong>, andramo soloina <strong>"he/she"</strong> na <strong>"him/her"</strong> ilay olona.</li>
        <li>Raha <strong>"he/she"</strong> → <strong>"who"</strong>. Raha <strong>"him/her"</strong> → <strong>"whom"</strong>.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "Who" = he/she (mpanao), "Whom" = him/her (iharan'ny atao). "To whom" = amin'iza, "Of whom" = an'iza, "For whom" = ho an'iza.
