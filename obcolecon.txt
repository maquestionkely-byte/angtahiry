  <!-- Carte : Inona ny Object Complements ?                        -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "Object Complements" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>Object Complements</strong> dia <strong>teny na andian-teny</strong> izay <strong>manome fampahalalana fanampiny</strong> momba ny zavatra iainan'ny matoanteny (verb).<br><br>
      🔹 Misy karazany roa lehibe :<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Direct Object (DO)</strong> → zavatra <strong>mivantana</strong> voakasiky ny asa atao.<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Indirect Object (IO)</strong> → olona na zavatra <strong>voataona</strong> na <strong>tompoin'ny asa</strong>.<br><br>
      <strong>🔹 Fanontaniana ahafantarana :</strong><br>
      • <strong>Direct Object</strong> → mamaly ny <strong>"Inona?"</strong> na <strong>"Iza?"</strong> (What? / Who?)<br>
      • <strong>Indirect Object</strong> → mamaly ny <strong>"Ho an'iza?"</strong> na <strong>"Ho an'inona?"</strong> (To whom? / For whom? / To what?)
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 1. DIRECT OBJECT – Zavatra mivantana                        -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "DIRECT OBJECT" – Zavatra mivantana <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Zavatra voakasika mivantana)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>Direct Object</strong> dia <strong>zavatra na olona</strong> izay <strong>mivantana</strong> voakasiky ny asa ataon'ny matoanteny.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fanontaniana :</strong> <strong>"Inona?"</strong> na <strong>"Iza?"</strong> (What? / Who?)<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • I eat <strong class="color-do">an apple</strong>. → (Inona no hohaniko? <strong class="color-do">An apple</strong>)<br>
      • She sees <strong class="color-do">him</strong>. → (Iza no hitany? <strong class="color-do">Him</strong>)<br>
      • They bought <strong class="color-do">a car</strong>. → (Inona no novidiny? <strong class="color-do">A car</strong>)
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🎯</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I eat <strong class="color-do">an apple</strong>. → Mihinana <strong class="color-do">paoma</strong> aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">She reads <strong class="color-do">a book</strong>. → Mamaky <strong class="color-do">boky</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">They love <strong class="color-do">her</strong>. → Tia <strong class="color-do">azy</strong> izy ireo.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">He bought <strong class="color-do">a house</strong>. → Nividy <strong class="color-do">trano</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I see <strong class="color-do">you</strong>. → Mahita <strong class="color-do">anao</strong> aho.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight-do">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <strong>Direct Object</strong> dia mamaly ny fanontaniana <strong>"Inona?"</strong> na <strong>"Iza?"</strong>. Izy no zavatra <strong>mivantana</strong> voakasiky ny asa.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 2. INDIRECT OBJECT – Zavatra tsy mivantana                  -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>2️⃣ "INDIRECT OBJECT" – Zavatra tsy mivantana <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#b45309;font-weight:400;">(Olona na zavatra voataona)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      Ny <strong>Indirect Object</strong> dia <strong>olona na zavatra</strong> izay <strong>voataona</strong> na <strong>tompoin'ny asa</strong>, fa tsy voakasika mivantana.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fanontaniana :</strong> <strong>"Ho an'iza?"</strong> na <strong>"Ho an'inona?"</strong> (To whom? / For whom? / To what?)<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Ohatra :</strong><br>
      • I give <strong class="color-io">her</strong> a book. → (Ho an'iza no homeko boky? <strong class="color-io">Her</strong>)<br>
      • He sent <strong class="color-io">me</strong> a letter. → (Ho an'iza no nandefasany taratasy? <strong class="color-io">Me</strong>)<br>
      • She bought <strong class="color-io">him</strong> a gift. → (Ho an'iza no nividiany fanomezana? <strong class="color-io">Him</strong>)
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🎁</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I give <strong class="color-io">her</strong> a flower. → Manome <strong class="color-io">azy</strong> voninkazo aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">He sent <strong class="color-io">me</strong> an email. → Nandefa <strong class="color-io">ahy</strong> mailaka izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">She bought <strong class="color-io">him</strong> a gift. → Nividy <strong class="color-io">azy</strong> fanomezana izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">They told <strong class="color-io">us</strong> a story. → Nitantara <strong class="color-io">anay</strong> tantara izy ireo.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I cooked <strong class="color-io">you</strong> dinner. → Nahandro <strong class="color-io">anao</strong> sakafo hariva aho.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight-io">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <strong>Indirect Object</strong> dia mamaly ny fanontaniana <strong>"Ho an'iza?"</strong> na <strong>"Ho an'inona?"</strong>. Izy no <strong>voataona</strong> na <strong>tompoin'ny asa</strong>.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 3. COMPARAISON : DIRECT vs INDIRECT OBJECT                   -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📊 Fampitahana : Direct Object vs Indirect Object</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Tabilao famintinana :</strong>
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <table class="verb-table">
        <tr><th style="background:#1e40af;">Object</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Fanontaniana</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Dikany</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Ohatra</th></tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong class="color-do">Direct Object</strong></td>
          <td>Inona? / Iza?<br>(What? / Who?)</td>
          <td>Zavatra mivantana voakasika</td>
          <td>I eat <strong class="color-do">an apple</strong>.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong class="color-io">Indirect Object</strong></td>
          <td>Ho an'iza? / Ho an'inona?<br>(To whom? / For whom?)</td>
          <td>Olona na zavatra voataona</td>
          <td>I give <strong class="color-io">her</strong> a book.</td>
        </tr>
      </table>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong><br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-do">Direct Object</strong> → mamaly "Inona?" na "Iza?"<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-io">Indirect Object</strong> → mamaly "Ho an'iza?" na "Ho an'inona?"
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 4. FANAZAVANA : Misy matoanteny mila Direct Object ihany         -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Misy matoanteny mila Direct Object ihany</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      Ny matoanteny sasany dia <strong>mila Direct Object</strong> mba hahatonga azy ho feno dikany. Ireo dia antsoina hoe <strong>transitive verbs</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ohatra :</strong> <strong>eat, read, see, buy, love, want</strong><br><br>
      • I eat. → ❌ (tsy feno dikany)<br>
      • I eat <strong class="color-do">an apple</strong>. → ✅ (feno dikany)<br><br>
      Ny <strong>Indirect Object</strong> kosa dia <strong>tsy voatery</strong> amin'ny fehezanteny rehetra. Misy matoanteny mila azy, fa misy koa tsy mila.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>⚠️</span> Ohatra amin'ny matoanteny mila Direct Object
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">She <strong>reads</strong> <strong class="color-do">a magazine</strong>. → Mamaky <strong class="color-do">gazety</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">They <strong>want</strong> <strong class="color-do">water</strong>. → Mila <strong class="color-do">rano</strong> izy ireo.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">He <strong>bought</strong> <strong class="color-do">a new phone</strong>. → Nividy <strong class="color-do">telefaona vaovao</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny matoanteny sasany dia <strong>mila Direct Object</strong> mba ho feno dikany. Raha tsy misy azy, dia tsy lavorary ny fehezanteny.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 5. FANAZAVANA : Misy matoanteny afaka maka Direct sy Indirect   -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Misy matoanteny afaka maka Direct sy Indirect miaraka</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      Misy matoanteny sasany afaka maka <strong>Direct Object</strong> sy <strong>Indirect Object</strong> miaraka. Ireo dia antsoina hoe <strong>ditransitive verbs</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ohatra :</strong> <strong>give, send, tell, buy, show, teach</strong><br><br>
      <strong>Structure :</strong> <strong>Subject + Verb + Indirect Object + Direct Object</strong><br><br>
      • I give <strong class="color-io">her</strong> <strong class="color-do">a book</strong>.<br>
      • He sent <strong class="color-io">me</strong> <strong class="color-do">a letter</strong>.<br>
      • She showed <strong class="color-io">us</strong> <strong class="color-do">her house</strong>.
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>📦</span> Ohatra amin'ny ditransitive verbs
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I give <strong class="color-io">him</strong> <strong class="color-do">a gift</strong>. → Manome <strong class="color-io">azy</strong> <strong class="color-do">fanomezana</strong> aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">She sent <strong class="color-io">her mother</strong> <strong class="color-do">a letter</strong>. → Nandefa <strong class="color-io">ny reniny</strong> <strong class="color-do">taratasy</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">They told <strong class="color-io">us</strong> <strong class="color-do">the truth</strong>. → Nilaza <strong class="color-io">anay</strong> <strong class="color-do">ny marina</strong> izy ireo.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">He bought <strong class="color-io">his daughter</strong> <strong class="color-do">a bike</strong>. → Nividy <strong class="color-io">ny zanany vavy</strong> <strong class="color-do">bisikileta</strong> izy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">We showed <strong class="color-io">them</strong> <strong class="color-do">our photos</strong>. → Nasehonay <strong class="color-io">azy ireo</strong> <strong class="color-do">ny sarintsika</strong>.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Misy matoanteny afaka maka <strong>Direct</strong> sy <strong>Indirect</strong> miaraka (ohatra : give, send, tell, show). Ny <strong>Indirect Object</strong> dia eo alohan'ny <strong>Direct Object</strong>.
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 6. FANAZAVANA : PRONOUNS AMIN'NY OBJECT                      -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Pronouns amin'ny maha-Object</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      Rehefa <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronoun)</strong> no ampiasaina ho <strong>Object</strong>, dia tsy maintsy mampiasa ny <strong>object pronouns</strong>.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Object Pronouns :</strong><br>
      <strong>me, you, him, her, it, us, them</strong><br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Direct Object :</strong> I see <strong class="color-do">him</strong>.<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Indirect Object :</strong> I give <strong class="color-io">her</strong> a book.
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <table class="verb-table">
        <tr><th style="background:#1e40af;">Sujet (Subject)</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Objet (Object)</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Dikany</th></tr>
        <tr><td>I</td><td><strong class="color-do">me</strong></td><td>ahy</td></tr>
        <tr><td>You</td><td><strong class="color-do">you</strong></td><td>anao / anareo</td></tr>
        <tr><td>He</td><td><strong class="color-do">him</strong></td><td>azy (lehilahy)</td></tr>
        <tr><td>She</td><td><strong class="color-do">her</strong></td><td>azy (vehivavy)</td></tr>
        <tr><td>It</td><td><strong class="color-do">it</strong></td><td>izy / io (zavatra)</td></tr>
        <tr><td>We</td><td><strong class="color-do">us</strong></td><td>anay</td></tr>
        <tr><td>They</td><td><strong class="color-do">them</strong></td><td>azy ireo</td></tr>
      </table>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top:8px;">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Rehefa <strong>Object</strong> ny pronon, dia ampiasao ny <strong>object pronouns</strong> : me, you, him, her, it, us, them.
    </div>
  </div>
