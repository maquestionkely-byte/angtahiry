  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "reflexive pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>reflexive pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> ampiasaina rehefa <strong>mitovy ny mpanao (sujet)</strong> sy ny <strong>iharan'ny atao (objet)</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia <strong>miverina any amin'ilay mpanao</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"myself"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>I</strong> (tena miteny).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"yourself"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>you</strong> (tokana).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"himself"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>he</strong> (lahy).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"herself"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>she</strong> (vavy).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"ourselves"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>we</strong> (isika / izahay).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"yourselves"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>you</strong> (maro).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>"themselves"</strong> → ho an'ny <strong>they</strong> (izy ireo).<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Myself</strong> → tenako / izaho<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Yourself</strong> → tenanao / ianao<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Himself</strong> → tenany (lahy)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Herself</strong> → tenany (vavy)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ourselves</strong> → tenantsika / tenanay<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Yourselves</strong> → tenanareo<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Themselves</strong> → tenany ireo
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "MYSELF" – Tenako / Izaho <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(ho an'ny I)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Myself"</strong> dia ampiasaina rehefa <strong>"I"</strong> no mpanao ary <strong>"I"</strong> ihany no iharan'ny atao.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Tenako"</strong> na <strong>"izaho"</strong> (rehefa miverina amin'ny tena).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🔄</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I hurt <strong class="color-myself">myself</strong>. → Nandratra <strong>tenako</strong> aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I taught <strong class="color-myself">myself</strong> English. → Nampianatra <strong>tenako</strong> anglisy aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I looked at <strong class="color-myself">myself</strong> in the mirror. → Nijery <strong>tenako</strong> tao amin'ny fitaratra aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I can do it <strong class="color-myself">myself</strong>. → Afaka manao izany <strong>izaho</strong> irery aho.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I blame <strong class="color-myself">myself</strong>. → Mamelona <strong>tenako</strong> aho.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> Ny <strong>"myself"</strong> dia miaraka amin'ny <strong>"I"</strong> ihany. Ohatra : <em>I cut <strong>myself</strong>.</em>
    </div>
