Exercice : Types of Sentences - Niveau 2 (facile)

Consigne : Identifiez le type de chaque phrase.
A) Declarative / B) Interrogative / C) Imperative / D) Exclamatory

1. I don't like spinach.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra tsotra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy tiako ny epinara.

2. Have you finished your homework?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Vita ve ny entimodinao?

3. Please don't make noise.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Aza mamoaka tabataba, azafady.

B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
