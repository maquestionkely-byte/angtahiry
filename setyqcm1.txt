Exercice : Types of Sentences - Niveau 1 (très facile)

Consigne : Identifiez le type de chaque phrase.
A) Declarative / B) Interrogative / C) Imperative / D) Exclamatory

1. The sun is shining today.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara zava-misy tsotra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Mamirapiratra ny masoandro androany.
