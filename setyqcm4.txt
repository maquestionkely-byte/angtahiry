Exercice : Types of Sentences - Niveau 4 (difficile)

Consigne : Identifiez le type de chaque phrase.
A) Declarative / B) Interrogative / C) Imperative / D) Exclamatory

1. I wonder if he will finally come to this meeting tomorrow morning.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Manontany tena aho raha ho tonga amin'ity fivoriana ity rahampitso maraina izy.

2. Would you be so kind as to tell me the way to the station?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana fahalalam-pomba, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Azonao atao ve ny milaza amiko ny lalana mankany amin'ny gara?

3. Let no one move from here until I return.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Aza misy mihetsika eto mandra-piveriko.

4. What an incredible coincidence to run into you here, at the end of the world!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Tena fifandrifian-javatra mahatalanjona ny nifankahita anao eto, any amin'ny faran'izao tontolo izao!

5. No one truly knows what lies behind these thick walls.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara zava-misy, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy misy mahalala marina izay miafina any aorian'ireo rindrina matevina ireo.

6. Wouldn't it be wiser to wait a few more days?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Tsy ho hendry kokoa ve ny miandry andro vitsivitsy?

7. Never reveal this secret to anyone, no matter what they offer you.
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
*C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manome baiko, ka imperative.
TRADUCTION: Aza ambara mihitsy izao tsiambaratelo izao na olona na inona na inona atolotr'izy ireo anao.

8. How sad it is to see this historic building left abandoned!
A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
*D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mampiseho fihetsehampo, ka exclamatory.
TRADUCTION: Mampalahelo ny mahita ity trano manan-tantara ity nilaozana!

9. This explanation doesn't seem entirely convincing to me.
*A) Declarative
B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia manambara hevitra, ka declarative.
TRADUCTION: Ity fanazavana ity dia tsy dia mandresy lahatra ahy.

10. Why have you never dared to confess your true feelings to her?
A) Declarative
*B) Interrogative
C) Imperative
D) Exclamatory
EXPLICATION: Io fehezanteny io dia mametraka fanontaniana, ka interrogative.
TRADUCTION: Nahoana no tsy nanana herim-po nilaza taminy ny tena fihetseham-ponao mihitsy ianao?
