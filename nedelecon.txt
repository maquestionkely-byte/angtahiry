  <!-- Carte : Inona ny neuter demonstrative pronouns ?            -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "neuter demonstrative pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>neuter demonstrative pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> tsy milaza lahy na vavy (<strong>tsy misy maha-lahy na maha-vavy</strong>).<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia : <strong>this, that, it</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 <strong>"This"</strong> sy <strong>"that"</strong> dia <strong>mpanondro (démonstratifs)</strong>.<br>
      🔹 <strong>"It"</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronom personnel)</strong> ho an'ny zavatra tsy misy maha-lahy na vavy.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>This</strong> → zavatra <strong>akaiky</strong> (tokana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>That</strong> → zavatra <strong>lavitra</strong> (tokana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>It</strong> → <strong>zavatra efa resahina</strong> na zavatra tsy misy maha-lahy na vavy
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 1. THIS – zavatra akaiky                                     -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>1️⃣ "THIS" – Zavatra akaiky <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#1e40af;font-weight:400;">(Ity / Io)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>This</strong> dia ampiasaina hanondroana <strong>zavatra tokana (singulier)</strong> izay <strong>akaiky</strong> ilay miteny.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Ity"</strong> na <strong>"Io"</strong> (zavatra eo akaiky eo).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>👆</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is my book. → <strong>Ity</strong> no bokyko.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is beautiful. → <strong>Io</strong> no tsara tarehy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is mine. → <strong>Ity</strong> no ahy.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is a good idea. → <strong>Ity</strong> no hevitra tsara.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I like <strong class="color-this">this</strong>. → Tiako <strong>ity</strong>.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> <strong>"This"</strong> dia ho an'ny zavatra <strong>tokana</strong> sy <strong>akaiky</strong> anao. Ohatra : mitazona boky ianao dia milaza hoe "This is my book."
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 2. THAT – zavatra lavitra                                    -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>2️⃣ "THAT" – Zavatra lavitra <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#b45309;font-weight:400;">(Iny / Izy)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>That</strong> dia ampiasaina hanondroana <strong>zavatra tokana (singulier)</strong> izay <strong>lavitra</strong> ilay miteny.<br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Iny"</strong> na <strong>"Izy"</strong> (zavatra lavitra).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>👉</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is my car. → <strong>Iny</strong> no fiarako.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is expensive. → <strong>Izy</strong> no lafo.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is yours. → <strong>Iny</strong> no anao.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is far away. → <strong>Izy</strong> no lavitra.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I want <strong class="color-that">that</strong>. → Tiako <strong>iny</strong>.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> <strong>"That"</strong> dia ho an'ny zavatra <strong>tokana</strong> sy <strong>lavitra</strong> anao. Ohatra : manondro fiara lavitra ianao dia milaza hoe "That is my car."
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <!-- 3. IT – mpisolo ho an'ny zavatra tsy misy lahy na vavy      -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>3️⃣ "IT" – Mpisolo ho an'ny zavatra tsy misy lahy na vavy <span style="font-size:0.8rem;color:#0d9488;font-weight:400;">(Izy / Io)</span></h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>
      <strong>It</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronom personnel)</strong> ampiasaina hilazana:<br>
      • zavatra <strong>tsy misy maha-lahy na maha-vavy</strong> (zavatra, biby, hevitra...)<br>
      • zavatra <strong>efa resahina</strong> na <strong>hita maso</strong><br><br>
      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Izy"</strong> na <strong>"Io"</strong> (rehefa tsy olona).
    </div>

    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>🔹</span> Ohatra amin'ny teny anglisy sy malagasy
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is a book. → <strong>Izy</strong> dia boky.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is raining. → <strong>Io</strong> no orana (mamorona orana).</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I have a car. <strong class="color-it">It</strong> is red. → Manana fiara aho. <strong>Izy</strong> dia mena.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">Look at the cat. <strong class="color-it">It</strong> is sleeping. → Jereo ny saka. <strong>Izy</strong> no matory.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is important. → <strong>Izy</strong> dia zava-dehibe.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr">I love <strong class="color-it">it</strong>. → Tiako <strong>izy</strong>.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> <strong>"It"</strong> dia ampiasaina ho an'ny zavatra <strong>rehetra tsy olona</strong> (zavatra, biby, hevitra, toetrandro...). Tsy milaza lahy na vavy izy.
    </div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Fampitahana ny neuter demonstrative pronouns :</strong>
    </div>
    <div class="conjugaison-ligne">
      <table class="verb-table">
        <tr><th style="background:#1e40af;">Pronom</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Fonction</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Distance</th><th style="background:#1e40af;">Dikany malagasy</th></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-this">This</strong></td><td>Démonstratif (mpanondro)</td><td>Proche (akaiky)</td><td>Ity / Io</td></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-that">That</strong></td><td>Démonstratif (mpanondro)</td><td>Loin (lavitra)</td><td>Iny / Izy</td></tr>
        <tr><td><strong class="color-it">It</strong></td><td>Personnel (mpisolo olona)</td><td>Tsy misy (zavatra resahina)</td><td>Izy / Io</td></tr>
      </table>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight">
      💡 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong><br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-this">This</strong> → zavatra akaiky (tondroina)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-that">That</strong> → zavatra lavitra (tondroina)<br>
      ✅ <strong class="color-it">It</strong> → zavatra resahina na tsy misy lahy/vavy
    </div>

  <!-- 5. DIFFÉRENCE ENTRE this/that (démonstratifs) et it (pers.) -->
  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fahasamihafana : "This/That" vs "It"</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      <strong>"This"</strong> sy <strong>"That"</strong> dia <strong>mpanondro (démonstratifs)</strong> → ampiasaina <strong>hanondroana zavatra</strong> (mipetraka eo akaiky na lavitra).<br><br>
      <strong>"It"</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana (pronom personnel)</strong> → ampiasaina <strong>hisolo zavatra efa resahina</strong> na zavatra tsy mila tondroina intsony.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>This / That</strong> → <strong>mampiseho</strong> (tondroina amin'ny tanana na maso)<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>It</strong> → <strong>mamerina</strong> (zavatra efa fantatra)
    </div>
    <div class="exemple-modern">
      <div class="exo-titre">
        <span>⚖️</span> Fampitahana mazava
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is my phone. (tondroina ilay izy) → <strong>Ity</strong> no findayko.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is new. (miresaka ilay finday efa resahina) → <strong>Izy</strong> dia vaovao.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is your house. (tondroina lavitra) → <strong>Iny</strong> no tranonao.</span>
      </div>
      <div class="exo-ligne">
        <span class="fr"><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is big. (miresaka ilay trano efa resahina) → <strong>Izy</strong> dia lehibe.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is my favourite. → <strong>Ity</strong> no tiako indrindra.</li>
        <li><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is my brother. → <strong>Iny</strong> no rahalahiko.</li>
        <li><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is my dog. → <strong>Izy</strong> no aliko.</li>
        <li>I see a bird. <strong class="color-it">It</strong> is flying. → Mahita vorona aho. <strong>Izy</strong> no manidina.</li>
        <li><strong class="color-this">This</strong> is a problem. → <strong>Ity</strong> no olana.</li>
        <li><strong class="color-that">That</strong> is a solution. → <strong>Iny</strong> no vahaolana.</li>
        <li><strong class="color-it">It</strong> is easy. → <strong>Izy</strong> dia mora.</li>

  <!-- ============================================================ -->
  <div class="card">
    <h2>🎯 Torohevitra mahasoa ho an'ny mpianatra</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg" style="margin-bottom: 6px;">
      <ul class="liste-avec-lignes-vides">
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"This"</strong> rehefa <strong>akaiky</strong> anao ilay zavatra resahina.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"That"</strong> rehefa <strong>lavitra</strong> anao ilay zavatra resahina.</li>
        <li>Ampiasao ny <strong>"It"</strong> rehefa <strong>efa resahina</strong> ilay zavatra na rehefa tsy mila tondroina intsony.</li>
        <li><strong>"This"</strong> sy <strong>"that"</strong> dia <strong>mampiseho</strong> zavatra (tondroina amin'ny tanana na maso).</li>
        <li><strong>"It"</strong> dia <strong>mamerina</strong> zavatra efa fantatra.</li>
        <li>Tsarovy fa <strong>"it"</strong> dia ampiasaina ho an'ny zavatra rehetra <strong>tsy olona</strong> (zavatra, biby, hevitra, toetrandro).</li>
        <li>Aza afangaro ny <strong>"this"</strong> sy <strong>"that"</strong> amin'ny <strong>"it"</strong> : ny roa voalohany dia mpanondro, ny farany dia mpisolo.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <div class="highlight" style="margin-top: 8px;">
      🌱 <strong>Tsarovy :</strong> "This / That" = manondro (akaiky/lavitra). "It" = mamerina zavatra efa resahina. Io no fototry ny fampiasana azy ireo !
    </div>
  </div>

    function detectDevTools() {
      let devToolsOpen = false;
      const threshold = 160;
      setInterval(() => {
        const widthDiff = window.outerWidth - window.innerWidth;

        if (widthDiff > threshold || heightDiff > threshold) {
