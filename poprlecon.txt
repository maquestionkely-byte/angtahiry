    <h2>🏷️ Inona ny "possessive pronouns" ?</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      🔹 Ny <strong>possessive pronouns</strong> dia <strong>mpisolo anarana</strong> ampiasaina <strong>hanehoana ny fananana</strong>.<br><br>
      🔹 Izy ireo dia <strong>mijoro irery</strong> (tsy miaraka amin'ny anarana).<br><br>
      🔹 Ny <strong>possessive pronouns</strong> dia : <strong>mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs</strong>.<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Mine</strong> → an'ny olona miteny (izy tokana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Yours</strong> → an'ny olona iresahana (tokana na maro)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>His</strong> → an'ny lehilahy<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Hers</strong> → an'ny vehivavy<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Ours</strong> → an'ny olona miteny sy ny hafa (isika)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Theirs</strong> → an'ny olona hafa (izy ireo)
    </div>
  </div>

    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>📖 Règle :</strong><br>

      <strong>📌 Fampiasana amin'ny teny malagasy :</strong><br>
      <strong>"Ahy"</strong> na <strong>"An'ahy"</strong>.

  <div class="card">
    <h2>📌 Fahasamihafana : Adjectif possessif vs Pronom possessif</h2>
    <div class="definition-mg">
      <strong>🔹 Zava-dehibe tokony hotadidina :</strong><br><br>
      <strong>Adjectif possessif (mpamaritra)</strong> → miaraka amin'ny <strong>anarana (nom)</strong><br>
      <strong>Pronom possessif (mpisolo)</strong> → <strong>mijoro irery</strong> (tsy misy anarana)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Adjectif :</strong> This is <strong>my</strong> book. (Miaraka amin'ny "book")<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Pronom :</strong> This book is <strong>mine</strong>. (Mijoro irery)<br><br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Adjectif :</strong> These are <strong>your</strong> shoes. (Miaraka amin'ny "shoes")<br>
      <span class="icone">📌</span> <strong>Pronom :</strong> These shoes are <strong>yours</strong>. (Mijoro irery)
    </div>
